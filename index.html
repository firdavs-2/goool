Information About Football!

Soccer is a family of team sports that involve kicking the ball to score goals to varying degrees. Unsuccessfully, the word soccer usually refers to the form of football that is most popular in the place where the word is used. Sports commonly referred to as soccer include association football soccer, also called association football or soccer, is a game in which two teams of 11 players try to maneuver the ball into the opposing team's goal using any part of their body except their hands and arms. Only the goalkeeper can handle the ball and can do so only within the penalty area around the goal. The team with more goals wins. Football is the most popular ball game in the world in terms of number of participants and spectators. With its basic rules and necessary equipment, simple sports can be played almost anywhere: from official football fields (fields) to gyms, streets, school playgrounds, parks or beaches.
